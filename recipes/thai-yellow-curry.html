Thai Yellow Curry

Description

Creamy and delicious curry recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Yellow Onion, thinly sliced
  • 1lb Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • ⅓ Cup Yellow Curry Paste
  • 10 Baby Golden Yukon Potatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 14-ounce Can Coconut Cream
  • ½ Cup Water
  • Cilantro and Rice for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot over medium-low heat. Add onions and saute for a few minutes until fragrant and softened. Add chicken and curry paste; saute for 3-5 minutes. Add potatoes and stir to coat with the curry paste.
  2. Add coconut cream and water to the pot — simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the chicken and potatoes are fully cooked, adding more water depending on the consistency you want for the sauce.
  3. Serve over rice. Optionally stir in fish sauce or brown sugar.
