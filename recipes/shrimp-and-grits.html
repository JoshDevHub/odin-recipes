Shrimp and Grits

Description

Get a taste of the south with this rich shrimp and grits recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups Water
  • 2 tbsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Grits
  • 2 Cups Half-and-Half
  • 2lbs Uncooked Shrimp, peeled
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 pinch Cayenne Pepper
  • 1lb Andouille Sausage
  • 5 slices Bacon
  • 2 Bell Peppers, green and red
  • ¼ Cup Butter
  • ¼ Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Cup Chicken Broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 Cup Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring water and salt to a boil in a saucepan. Whisk grits into boiling water and add half-and-half. Reduce heat to medium-low and stir until grits are thickened. Set aside and keep warm.
  2. Sprinkle shrimp with salt and cayenne pepper.
  3. Brown sausage slices in a pan. Set aside when done.
  4. In the same pan used to brown the sausage, add bacon slices and fry for 5 minutes on each side. Remove to plate with paper towel to drain.
  5. Add bell pepper and onion to the pan with the bacon drippings. Cook and stir until onion is translucent — 8 minutes
  6. Stir cooked sausage and shrimp into the vegetable mixture and set aside.
  7. Use butter and flour in a saucepan to create a roux. The roux should be a golden brown color.
  8. Pour the roux over the shrimp, sausage, and vegetables and stir to combine. Add chicken broth, crumbled bacon, and Worcestershire and stir to combine. Cook until sauce thickens and shrimp becomes pink and opaque.
  9. Before serving, mix sharp cheddar cheese into grits. Serve shrimp mixture over bowl of grits.
